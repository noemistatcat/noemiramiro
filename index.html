About

Hi! I am a data scientist specializing in marketing analytics and business intelligence. In my 13 years working in the field, I have helped over 70 companies globally understand their business and customers through data.

These are the services that I can offer:
- Statistical Analysis using R, Python, SQL, SAS
- Survey analysis (Quantitative and Qualitative Methods)
- Dashboards and Data Visualization through Tableau, Google Data Studio, Looker, Sisense, and Powerpoint Presentations
- Data Engineering and ETL through Google Big Query, dbt, LookML, Snowflake, Apache Cassandra
